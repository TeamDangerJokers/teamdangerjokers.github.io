History of Team Deadly Jokers

The idea of ​​his studio, no matter small or large, appeared in Janis as a child. At the same time, the studio's name "TeamDeadlyJokers" or "TDJ" appeared. Why jokers? Because the favorite comic book character after Batman was the Joker.

All this is very cool, but then the knowledge of Janis was at the level of the GameMaker engine. And although the capabilities of this engine are not weak - Janis' ideas died with school years. This is how the very good project Mr.Jacke: Key for prices died. "Maybe the time will come and the world will see it," says Janis.

After graduating from school, Janis entered KROK University, where he studied graphics and design. In it he found a friend and future member of the team, Ruslan. Together with him, on the GameMakerStudio2 engine, before the open day, they created the game UniMaze . It was their term paper.

Not without problems. Initially, to launch the game at the presentation, the development team was given a computer that had more to do with dinosaurs than with a normal machine. UniMaze - 2D game consisting of two levels and has 5 moving elements, one of which is the main character. At the presentation, the car gave only 15 FPS, so I had to use my laptop, but it also brought some surprises. To explain to people how it works, it was necessary to constantly compile the project. The project cache filled the RAM and the laptop turned off. It took half an hour to restart and debug the damaged project file, but overall it was an interesting experience.

After defending the theoretical part of the course, the dean of the university offered to continue working on game development, and the team was joined by 3D modeler Arthur. That's how TeamDeadlyJokers

was born

Our headquarters currently consists of two permanent and three temporary participants.
